Our story

Disrupting the legal and finance industry through design-driven simplicity

Lawyers struggle to get paid. Excuses are common: clients claim they never got the invoice, they ran out of checks, or they just haven’t gotten around to paying. Widr Pay began with one mission: help lawyers get paid quickly.

Increased payment delivery through seamless billing integration

Widr Pay cuts the average time it takes for a lawyer to get paid from 90 days to 13 days. It does this by making it easy for lawyers to use and every easier for clients to pay.

Invoices reimagined

Fluid design and workflow

Rethinking how people pay and get paid

Unlike most billing systems used by law firms, Widr Pay accepts all forms of payment: from Apple and Google Pay to SEPA. This takes the payment pain point that most clients experience, and gives them an effortless way to pay.

Created for large and small firms alike

Widr Pay is an interface great for small firms, but that grows as they grow; with thoughtful design, Widr Pay ensures that it could scale with the business.

The future of legal industry

The world has changed

Global pandemics have made people uncomfortable with paper checks. Widr Pay takes law firms into the post-pandemic world with paperless payment.

The solo lawyer

The gig economy has changed the way lawyers do business. Lawyers are finding that they can become more profitable moving away from large firms and becoming solo lawyers.

The team

A global collaboration of creators and thinkers

Widr Pay is a diverse team of international innovators. They are doers, dreamers, designers, and developers. United, they are creating products that disrupt the industry and change the world.
